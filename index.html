AI Project: ReelRatings

A semester-long mini project for an Artificial Intelligence class. My teammates and I developed a movie recommendation analyzer that uses sentiment analysis to help users determine if movie reviews carry a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. Sentiment analysis is a great innovative tool to understand what users think and feel about certain products or services, and can be a key component in leading marketing research and tactics. Learn more about the project here and view the code for the project on GitLab.

Slow Fashion is Just Fast Fashion In Denial

A blog post published on Medium.com discussing how marketing tactics have changed the meaning of slow fashion to promote overconsumption. Slow fashion is what started as a movement to think before you buy new clothing; that definition has been heavily altered since then.

Building Reading Habits With Notion

Stuck in that dreadful loop of wanting to read more books, but never actually reading them? Notion's got you covered (and so do I). In this YouTube video, I demonstrate how to build a personalized virtual library to keep you organized and motivated to read, in just a few simple steps. I love to read and love to stay organized, and Notion makes it possible to achieve both!
